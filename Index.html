The Plan at a Glance

You're already perfectly positioned in Colmar — the closest French resorts are about 1.5 hrs away, with Swiss options up to 2.5 hrs. Pick one base below, arrive Dec 27, and spend the week learning to ski as a family before ringing in the New Year on the mountain. Costs run roughly €2,975–3,175 for the group in France (Switzerland adds €300–500).

Essential Planning

Everything You Need
to Know

Logistics, tips, booking reminders, and family-friendly advice.

🎟 Book These NOW

  • Tower of London — sells out Dec weekends
  • Notre-Dame — timed entry books weeks ahead
  • Sainte-Chapelle — popular at Christmas
  • Eiffel Tower summit — 3–4 weeks ahead
  • Louvre — timed tickets recommended
  • Versailles — book with entry time slot
  • Hyde Park Ice Rink — book 48hrs ahead

✈ Getting There

  • London: fly direct from US to Heathrow
  • London → Basel: ~1.5hr flight to BSL
  • Basel → Colmar: 45 min taxi or bus
  • Colmar → Paris: TGV ~2.5hrs via Strasbourg
  • Within Paris: Métro is fast & cheap
  • Versailles: RER C train ~35 min

🌡 Weather to Expect

  • London: 3–8°C, grey but festive
  • Alsace: 0–5°C, possible snow ✨
  • Paris: 3–7°C, crisp and clear
  • Pack: heavy coats, warm boots, gloves
  • Tip: hand warmers for market days!

👧 Kids Ages 9 & 11

  • Perfect ages — old enough to love history
  • Young enough to believe in magic
  • Give each a small travel journal
  • Budget daily "hot chocolate money"
  • Colmar: collect a stamp from each market
  • Beefeater tour will be a highlight

🏨 Where to Stay

  • London: South Bank or Westminster
  • Colmar: Old Town — book ASAP for Dec
  • Try: Hôtel le Maréchal (canal-front)
  • Paris: 4th arrondissement (Marais)
  • Walking distance to Notre-Dame

🍽 Food You Must Try

  • London: Borough Market, hot chestnuts
  • Alsace: Flammekueche, Bredele, Glühwein
  • Alsace: Choucroute, pain d'épices
  • Paris: croissants daily, crème brûlée
  • Paris: hot chocolate at Angelina

💶 Money Tips

  • UK uses £GBP, France uses €EUR
  • Paris Museum Pass saves on entries
  • Under-18s free at many Paris museums
  • Christmas markets: mostly free entry
  • Carry cash for market stalls

📸 Best Photo Spots

  • Colmar canal at night — pure magic
  • Sainte-Chapelle stained glass
  • Eiffel Tower from Trocadéro at dusk
  • Tower Bridge from the South Bank
  • Versailles Hall of Mirrors
